What is the Adhara Starpath?

In a few lines, our goal is to rescue the magical knowledge of the Stars, catalog their Magic and work with them for improving ourselves and the world in which we live.

This Path was built so that anyone, of any belief, spirituality or religion, can be part of, learn from and include their personal connection with the stars in their spiritual practices.
