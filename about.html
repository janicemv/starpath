About Adhara Starpath

Adhara Starpath is one of the ways to access the Wisdom of the Stars. It is based on our connection with the Royal Stars. They are: Fomalhaut, Aldebaran, Regulus, Antares and Sirius.

The first four stars were called Royal Stars or Grigori in Mesopotamian star cults. Sirius enters as the fifth Royal Star due to the personal perceptions that I and the people who accompanied me in conducting various workshops and magical processes since 2018.

Our objective is, in a few lines, to rescue the magical knowledge of the Stars, catalog their Magic and bring it to improve ourselves and the world in which we live. By doing this, we are also aligning ourselves with our Divine Essence, our Soul purpose, and thus we can be better agents of Transformation around us.

The Adhara Path is a system of connection with the Stars independent of belief or religion. We respect everyone's individual spirituality and seek to learn from each other while creating a common language of contact with the Universe. ðŸ’–

Goals of the Adhara Starpath

  1. Catalog the magical characteristics of known constellations and stars (and get to know the unknown ones);
  2. Develop practical magical work with the stars and stellar glyphs;
  3. Understand the role of stars in our world and creation;
  4. Work with dimensions and planes of existence;
  5. Work magically with the stars and other celestial bodies;
  6. Study about webs and stellar temples;
  7. Get in touch with stellar beings and people and have them as magical allies;
  8. Bringing the individual closer to their essence/divine Self, understanding their sacred contracts for a better life and to help make our world better;
  9. Promote courses and disseminate acquired knowledge;
  10. Bring the blessings of the stars to this plane of existence for the benefit of the members of the path and the world;
  11. Integrate the individual with their Light and Shadow for their balance and personal development;
  12. Bringing people from different paths together towards a common goal;
  13. Live in Truth, Love and Honesty.
